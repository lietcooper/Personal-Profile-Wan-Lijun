Nanchang Foreign Language School

Nanchang Foreign Language School, referred to as "NFLS", is the Nanchang City Bureau of Education in charge of the public full-time complete secondary school, Jiangxi Province, the first public foreign language school, approved by the Ministry of Education of the country's sixteen foreign language schools with the qualification of direct admission to University.

Nanchang Foreign Language School was founded in 1994, its predecessor is the twenty-second secondary school in Nanchang, founded in 1970; in 1994, approved by the Nanchang Municipal People's Government, the name was changed to Nanchang Foreign Language School; in 2002, the school moved to Taoyuan; November 2005, the school was approved by the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China as a foreign language school with the qualification of guaranteed admission to colleges and universities; in January 2017, the Nanchang Foreign Language School Jiulonghu Campus was officially opened.

According to Nanchang Foreign Language School 2020 enrollment briefings, Nanchang Foreign Language School Taoyuan Campus covers an area of more than 60 acres, with a floor area of 42,631 square meters; Jiulonghu Campus covers an area of 175 acres; the school Taoyuan Campus Junior High School has 30 teaching classes, with 1,900 students, and Jiulonghu Campus Senior High School has 39 teaching classes, with 1,950 students.
