Southeast University

Southeast University, with the main campus located in Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, is the People's Republic of China directly under the Ministry of Education of the national key universities, ranked in the country's "double first-class" category A. "985 project", "211 project", selected as "2011 program", "111 program", 985 Project" and "211 Project", selected for the "2011 Program", "111 Program", "Excellent Engineer Education and Training Program", "Excellence in Education and Training Program" and "Excellence in Education and Training Program", as well as the "2011 Program", "2011 Program" and "111 Program". It has been selected as one of the "2011 Plan", "111 Plan", "Excellent Engineer Education and Training Program", "Excellent Doctor Education and Training Program", "National Innovative Experimental Program for College Students", "National Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program for College Students", "National Program for Sending Postgraduates to High-ranking Universities", "National Demonstration University for Deepening Innovation and Entrepreneurship Education Reform", and "Degree Authorization Autonomous Audit Unit", which is built by the Ministry of Education of China and the Jiangsu Province.

The school was founded in 1902 in Sanjiang Normal Hall, after two rivers Normal Hall, Nanjing Higher Normal School, National Southeast University, National Central University and other important development period. 1952 national university faculty restructuring, the school of arts and science and other subjects out of the original National Central University College of Engineering as the main body, has been merged into the Fudan University, Jiaotong University, Zhejiang University, Jinling University and other schools of the relevant departments in the original site of the National Central University headquarters established Nanjing College of Technology. In May 1988, the school was renamed Southeast University. in April 2000, the former Southeast University, Nanjing Railway Medical College, Nanjing Transportation College of Higher Education merged with Nanjing Geological School to form a new Southeast University.

As of April 2020, Southeast University has built the campuses of Sipailou, Jiulonghu, and Dingjiaqiao, covering an area of 5888 mu, of which Jiulonghu Campus is 3,752.35 mu, with a total floor area of about 789,700 square meters; there are 2,991 full-time faculty members; 1,071 supervisors for doctoral degree students and 2,166 supervisors for master's degree students. There are 12 academicians from both academies; 34 faculties and departments and 83 undergraduate majors; 34 doctoral degree first-level subject authorization points and 48 master's degree first-level subject authorization points; and 36,277 full-time students, including 16,200 undergraduates and 2,007 graduate students.
