Nanchang

Nanchang, abbreviated as "Hong" or "Chang" and known as Yuzhang and Hongdu in ancient times, is the capital of Jiangxi Province, the core city of the city cluster around Poyang Lake, and an important central city in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River in China as approved by the State Council. As of 2019, the city has six districts and three counties under its jurisdiction, with a total area of 7,195 square kilometers . According to the seventh census, the resident population of Nanchang is 625,5007.

Nanchang is located in east China, north of central Jiangxi Province, downstream of Gan River and Fu River, and southwest shore of Poyang Lake. Nanchang is the political, economic, cultural, scientific and educational, and transportation center of Jiangxi Province, and is known as "Guangdong Households and Fujian Courtyards, the Head of Wu and the Tail of Chu", "Lapel of Three Rivers and Five Lakes", and the place of "controlling barbarians and thorns and attracting Ouyue". Nanchang is the only provincial capital city in China that adjoins the Yangtze River Delta, the Pearl River Delta, and the West Coast of the Taiwan Straits Economic Zone, and it is also one of the important central cities of east China, and the center city of the city cluster of the Middle reaches of the Yangtze River.

Nanchang is a national historical and cultural city, named for "Changda Nanjiang, Southern Prosperity", the early Tang Dynasty Four Heroes Wang Bo in the "Preface to the Tengwang Pavilion" said it is "a place of great wealth, great talent"; South Tang Dynasty Nanchang Province known as the "Nandu"; the 1927 Nanchang Uprising of August 1, where the Communist Party of China gave birth to the first independently-led People's Army, is a famous revolutionary heroes of the city, known as the place where the flag was raised; the founding of New China, Nanchang manufactured the first aircraft in China, the first batch of sea defense missiles, the first motorcycle, tractor, is China's major manufacturing centers, the birthplace of new China's aviation industry!
