Nanchang Youzheng Rd Elementary School

Nanchang Youzheng Rd Elementary School was founded in the late Qing Guangxu, Nanchang City, one of the earliest founding of the elementary school, and all the centuries-old school, as she walked through the centuries-old road is also a brilliant school history.

The school adopts "Honesty, Scholarship, and Health" as its motto and aims to become a prestigious school with a century-old tradition of nurturing individuals. By integrating practical approaches, it diligently works towards constructing an education system known as the "Tree Culture," which focuses on establishing the school's brand, shaping the teachers' image, and cultivating students' new ethos. "Under the ancient charm of maple and poplar trees, culture is passed down; amidst the silent dedication of gardeners, the future is nurtured among the blossoms of peach and plum trees" truly reflects the "Red Candle Spirit" of generations at Youzheng Rd Elementary School.
